THE ORIGINAL TEXT READER

poetry+language>academic_essays>short_essays

on beginnings
ARE YOU READY? yes
* keep your hands and feet inside the vehicle
* you can't move back
* i'll see you on the other side
* have fun!
And in the best of all possible lives,
that beginning and that end are the same
